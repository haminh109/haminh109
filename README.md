## About Me

I'm **Ha Quang Minh**, a student in **Data Science in Economics and Business** with a strong interest in:

- **Artificial Intelligence**
- **Machine Learning**
- **Econometrics**
- **Time Series Forecasting**
- **Fraud Detection**
- **Research-Driven Engineering**

I enjoy working on problems where **data**, **models**, and **real decisions** meet.  
My goal is to build systems that are not only technically sound, but also useful in practice.

- **Macroeconomic forecasting** and real-time nowcasting
- **Fraud detection** and risk analytics
- **Machine learning for structured and tabular data**
- **Research workflows** with strong reproducibility
- **Applied AI systems** for decision support
